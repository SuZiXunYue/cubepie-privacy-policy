引言

欢迎使用 魔方派(CubePie)（以下简称"本应用"）。我们深知您的隐私对您的重要性，并致力于保护您的个人信息安全。本隐私政策旨在向您说明我们如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的信息。

1. 信息收集

1.1 我们收集的信息

本应用是一款魔方助手工具，我们的设计原则是尽可能减少数据收集。目前，本应用：

  • 不收集个人身份信息：我们不需要您注册账户或提供姓名、邮箱、电话号码等个人信息。
  • 不收集设备标识符：我们不收集设备ID、IDFA（广告标识符）或其他唯一设备标识符。
  • 不进行网络数据传输：应用的核心功能（魔方可视化、求解算法、计时功能）均在您的设备本地运行，无需网络连接。

1.2 本地存储的数据

以下数据仅存储在您的设备本地，不会上传至任何服务器：

  • 计时成绩记录（时间、日期）
  • 应用偏好设置（如选择的求解算法）

您可以随时通过清除应用数据来删除这些本地存储的信息。

2. 权限使用说明

为了提供完整的应用功能，本应用可能请求以下权限：

权限 用途 是否必须
相机 用于扫描魔方状态（功能开发中） 否
振动 用于计时器触觉反馈 否

说明：

  • 相机权限仅用于魔方扫描功能，不会拍摄、存储或传输任何照片或视频。
  • 您可以拒绝授予这些权限，应用的其他功能仍可正常使用。

3. 信息共享

我们不与任何第三方共享您的信息，原因很简单：我们不收集任何个人数据。

  • 本应用不集成任何第三方分析工具
  • 本应用不包含任何广告SDK
  • 本应用不使用社交媒体插件
  • 本应用不进行任何形式的数据出售或交易

4. 数据安全

由于本应用的所有数据均存储在您的设备本地，数据的安全性取决于您设备的安全措施。我们建议您：

  • 保持设备操作系统更新
  • 使用设备锁屏密码或生物识别锁
  • 不要对设备进行越狱或获取Root权限

5. 儿童隐私保护

本应用适合所有年龄段用户使用，包括13岁以下儿童。由于我们不收集任何个人数据，因此无需担心儿童个人信息被收集的问题。家长可以放心让孩子使用本应用学习魔方。

6. 您的权利

根据适用的数据保护法律，您享有以下权利：

  • 访问权：查看存储在设备上的本地数据
  • 删除权：通过清除应用数据删除所有本地存储信息
  • 知情权：了解应用的数据处理方式（即本隐私政策）

7. 隐私政策的变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。重大变更将通过应用内通知或应用商店更新说明告知您。建议您定期查阅本政策。

About Us

"We" in this Privacy Policy refers to the developer of the CubePie application. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us using the information provided at the end of this policy.

Introduction

Welcome to CubePie (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We understand the importance of your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

1. Information Collection

1.1 Information We Collect

CubePie is a Rubik's Cube assistant tool designed with the principle of minimizing data collection. Currently, the App:

  • Does not collect personal identification information: We do not require you to register an account or provide personal information such as name, email, or phone number.
  • Does not collect device identifiers: We do not collect device ID, IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers), or other unique device identifiers.
  • Does not perform network data transmission: The core features (cube visualization, solving algorithms, timer functionality) run entirely locally on your device without requiring an internet connection.

1.2 Locally Stored Data

The following data is stored only locally on your device and is not uploaded to any server:

  • Timer performance records (time, date)
  • App preference settings (e.g., selected solving algorithm)

You can delete this locally stored information at any time by clearing the app data.

2. Permission Usage

To provide complete app functionality, CubePie may request the following permissions:

Permission Purpose Required
Camera Used for scanning cube state (feature in development) No
Vibration Used for timer haptic feedback No

Note:

  • Camera permission is only used for the cube scanning feature and will not capture, store, or transmit any photos or videos.
  • You may decline these permissions, and other app features will continue to function normally.

3. Information Sharing

We do not share any information with third parties, simply because we do not collect any personal data.

  • The App does not integrate any third-party analytics tools
  • The App does not contain any advertising SDKs
  • The App does not use social media plugins
  • The App does not engage in any form of data selling or trading

4. Data Security

Since all app data is stored locally on your device, data security depends on your device's security measures. We recommend that you:

  • Keep your device's operating system updated
  • Use a device lock screen password or biometric lock
  • Do not jailbreak or root your device

5. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no concern about children's personal information being collected. Parents can confidently let their children use the App to learn Rubik's Cube.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: View locally stored data on your device
  • Right to Deletion: Delete all locally stored information by clearing app data
  • Right to Information: Understand how the app handles data (this Privacy Policy)

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through in-app notifications or App Store update notes. We recommend reviewing this policy regularly.
